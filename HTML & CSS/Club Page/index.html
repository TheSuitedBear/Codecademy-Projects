Hastings Running Club

Running since 1990

We are the Hastings Running Club!

Established in 1990, we aim to bring people from all walks of life together for one cause, better health for all with fitness!

Supported by all!

We've been supported by numerous local businesses in what we do, helping to inspire us to continue our mission on providing accessible fitness to all!

Want to join?

We meet up on fridays every week at 6pm! Our starting point is always outside of the town hall on 123 Fake Street!

We hope to see you there!
